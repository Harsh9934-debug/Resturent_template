About Noir Aurelia

Classic European heritage, reimagined for the modern connoisseur.

Noir Aurelia was born from a devotion to the grand dining rooms of old Europe—where every detail mattered, every course told a story, and every glass of wine was poured with reverence. Our philosophy is simple: to create an evening that lingers in memory long after the final candle fades.

From hand-polished crystal and bespoke porcelain to a cellar curated from the finest French, Italian, and Spanish estates, each element has been chosen to honor the timeless dance of cuisine, craft, and ceremony.

Chef’s Special

“Nuit d'Or” — a choreographed symphony of twelve courses.

The pinnacle of the Noir Aurelia experience, "Nuit d'Or" is an intimate journey through the chef’s most treasured inspirations. From the first whisper of amuse-bouche to the final mignardise, every bite has been envisioned to evoke memory, emotion, and wonder.

  • Highlights
  • Hand-dived scallops with champagne beurre blanc and Oscietra caviar.
  • Milk-fed lamb saddle, black garlic, confit shallot, and rosemary jus.
  • Tableside flamed Grand Marnier crêpes with Tahitian vanilla ice cream.

Exclusively available Friday and Saturday evenings. Reservations are confirmed after a personal concierge call.
